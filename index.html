Why This Matters

Smoke inhalation is the leading cause of residential fire fatalities. Every second counts.

Global Impact

~6.7M fires each year worldwide with ~170k deaths and $250B+ economic loss.

Source: International Association of Fire and Rescue Services.

China Data

~235k fires per year, 1,025 deaths, 2,665 injuries, and 3.86B yuan direct losses.

Source: Fire & Rescue Bureau, Ministry of Emergency Management.

Survival Window

For every 1 second of advance warning, survival can increase by 12%.

Source: Fire Science Research Institute.
